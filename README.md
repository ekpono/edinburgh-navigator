# Edinburgh Navigator

> Practical help for Edinburgh's 530,680+ residents and visitors — housing rights, NHS Lothian, Scottish benefits, transport, and council services, all in one place.

**Live:** [edinburgh-navigator.vercel.app](https://edinburgh-navigator.vercel.app)

---

## What it covers

| Section | What you'll find |
|---|---|
| Crisis Support | Emergency lines, legal rights, immediate help |
| Housing | Private Residential Tenancy law, deposits, eviction protections |
| Health & NHS | NHS Lothian, GP registration, prescriptions, mental health |
| Transport | Lothian Buses, trams, cycling, Edinburgh Airport |
| Employment | Jobs, Scottish benefits, training |
| Neighbourhoods | Compare areas, rents, and lifestyle |
| Clean Edinburgh | Bins, recycling, reporting problems |
| Community | Groups, events, faith |
| Youth & Rights | Under-22 free buses, EMA, young person support |
| Tax & Budget | Council tax, reductions, financial help |
| Visitor Guide | Attractions, day trips, practical tips |
| Council FAQs | Searchable answers to common council questions |

---

## AI search

The homepage includes an AI-powered assistant (Claude) that can answer questions about any of the above. Ask in plain English — e.g. *"How do I get my deposit back?"* or *"What free prescriptions am I entitled to?"*

## Scottish law note

Scotland has a separate legal system, NHS, and benefits structure from England and Wales. Key differences covered in this app:

- Prescriptions are **free**
- No-fault eviction is **banned** under the Private Residential Tenancy
- Many benefits are administered by **Social Security Scotland**, not the DWP
